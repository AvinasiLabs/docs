# Guides Overview

Practical guides for participating in the DeLong Protocol ecosystem.

## Participating in IDOs

Learn how to purchase tokens during Initial Data Offerings, evaluate pricing and timing, and understand the fundraising mechanics.

## Launching Datasets

Understand how to tokenize datasets, configure IDO parameters, and manage the launch process from deployment to liquidity creation.

## Accessing Datasets

Discover how to purchase rental hours, verify access permissions, and integrate dataset access into research workflows.

## Governance Participation

Learn how to create proposals, vote on treasury allocations and operational changes, and participate in protocol governance.

## Trading Tokens

Understand secondary market trading on Uniswap, liquidity dynamics, and token transfer mechanics.
